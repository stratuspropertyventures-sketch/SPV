About Us

An educational company, first.

Stratus Property Ventures exists to help people who are new to real estate investing get into their first deal — with a real understanding of the numbers, not just enthusiasm.

Why We Do This

Getting started in real estate investing is harder than it should be. The math is learnable, but most first-time investors never see it laid out clearly — so the first deal keeps getting pushed to "someday." We built Stratus Property Ventures to change that: to teach the underwriting, financing, and operating decisions that go into a good rental property, so a first-time investor can evaluate a deal with the same confidence as someone who's done it ten times.

What We Do

Teach the Fundamentals

Cash flow, cap rate, financing, depreciation, and risk — explained in plain terms, grounded in real numbers rather than rules of thumb.

Build the Tools

Our rental calculator models long-term and short-term rentals with the same rigor a professional investor would use to underwrite a deal.

Walk Through Real Deals

We help apply the numbers to actual properties — so a first-time investor isn't learning in the abstract.

Where We're Headed

Beyond the calculator, we're building out educational content — videos and a podcast — to walk through real deals, common mistakes, and the reasoning behind the numbers. The goal is the same throughout: make the path to a first rental property clear enough that anyone willing to learn can take it.
