Why We Exist

Real estate investing shouldn't require insider knowledge.

Most people who want to invest in rental property don't have a mentor, a family portfolio, or a background in finance to lean on. The numbers feel opaque, the risks feel unclear, and the first deal never happens. We started Stratus Property Ventures to close that gap — teaching the same underwriting discipline we use ourselves, so a first-time investor can walk into a deal with real understanding instead of guesswork.
